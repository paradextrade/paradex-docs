## Overview

The Taker pays **6bps of underlying notional** as a taker fee.

The Maker receives **1bp of underlying notional** as the maker rebate.

### Example 

Alice (taker) trades 2 BTC-USD-90000-C against Bob (maker). 
BTC spot price is 100000. 

Alice pays $$ 0.0006*2*100000=\$120 $$ and Bob receives $$ 0.0001*2*100000=\$20 $$
